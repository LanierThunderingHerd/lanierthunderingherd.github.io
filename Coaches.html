Coaches

Daryl Beard

Daryl Beard is the head coach for the boys track and field team. Coach Beard has over 6 years of coaching experience overall and 3 years of being the head coach. Coach Beard also has experience coaching the ATL Zoom Youth Track club for 3 years. He studied at North Carolina State University with a Master’s Degree in Education and obtained a USATF Level I Certification.He was awarded Region 8 GA Runner Up in 2018, Region 8 Championship in 2019, GA State Championship in 300MH, and the GA Track and Field Finals 2017, 2018, and 2019. Coach Beard’s family consists of his wife Tasha, and his kids, Kaden and Kennedy.

Herb Jones

Herb Jones is the coach for hurdles, long sprinting and endurance (mid-distance / distance) student-athletes. He provided training guidance that helped lead Zachary Charles to be crowned as the 300-meter hurdles 6A 2019 Georgia High School Association State Championship gold medalist and Nahil Perkins to become the runner-up silver medalist in the same race. Additionally, he is certified in the following:

  • USTFCCCA Certified Sprints, Hurdles, & Relays Coaching
  • USTFCCCA Certified in Track & Field Technical Coaching
  • Georgia High School Association (GHSA) Certified Community Coaching
